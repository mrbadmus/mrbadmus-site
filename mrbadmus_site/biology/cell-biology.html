🎯 Quick Quiz
1. Which organelle is found in plant cells but NOT animal cells?
Correct! Chloroplasts are only found in plant cells — they carry out photosynthesis ✅
Not quite. Chloroplasts are found only in plant cells. Nucleus, mitochondria and ribosomes are in both.
2. A cell image measures 5 mm. The actual cell is 0.05 mm. What is the magnification?
Correct! M = 5 ÷ 0.05 = ×100 ✅
Not quite. Magnification = image size ÷ actual size = 5 ÷ 0.05 = ×100
3. Which process moves substances from HIGH to LOW concentration without using energy?
Correct! Diffusion moves substances down a concentration gradient — no energy needed ✅
Not quite. Diffusion is the passive movement from high to low concentration. Active transport uses energy and goes against the gradient.
