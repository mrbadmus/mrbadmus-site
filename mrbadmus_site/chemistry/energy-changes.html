🧪 Energy Changes

Spec 4.5 Paper 1
📋 What You Need to Know
  • ● Exothermic and endothermic reactions
  • ● Reaction profiles — activation energy
  • ● Bond energies — breaking bonds (endothermic) vs forming bonds (exothermic)
  • ● Cells and batteries
  • ● Fuel cells — hydrogen fuel cell
📐 Key Equations
ΔH = energy in (breaking) − energy out (forming)
⚽ FIFA Worked Example
⚽ FIFA Example

In a reaction, 800 kJ/mol of energy is needed to break bonds and 950 kJ/mol is released when bonds form. Is the reaction exothermic or endothermic? Calculate ΔH.

F

ΔH = energy in (breaking bonds) − energy out (forming bonds)

I

ΔH = 800 − 950

F

Negative ΔH = exothermic

A

ΔH = −150 kJ/mol → Exothermic reaction
