A radioactive sample has an initial count rate of 800 counts/min. Its half-life is 20 minutes. What is the count rate after 60 minutes?

F

Each half-life → count rate halves

I

60 min ÷ 20 min = 3 half-lives

F

800 → 400 → 200 → 100

A

Count rate = 100 counts/min
