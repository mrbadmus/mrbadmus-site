⚽ FIFA Example

A transformer has 200 turns on the primary and 1000 turns on the secondary. The primary voltage is 230 V. Find the secondary voltage.

F

Vp/Vs = Np/Ns → Vs = Vp × (Ns/Np)

I

Vs = 230 × (1000/200)

F

Vs = 230 × 5

A

Vs = 1150 V
