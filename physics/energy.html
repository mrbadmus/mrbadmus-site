🎯 Quick Quiz
1. A 5 kg object moves at 4 m/s. Calculate its kinetic energy.
Correct! KE = ½mv² = ½ × 5 × 16 = 40 J ✅
Not quite. KE = ½mv² = ½ × 5 × 4² = ½ × 5 × 16 = 40 J
2. What is the unit of energy?
Correct! Energy is measured in Joules (J) ✅
Not quite. Energy is measured in Joules (J). Watts = power, Newtons = force.
3. Which statement about energy is always true?
Correct! The law of conservation of energy — energy is never created or destroyed, only transferred ✅
Not quite. Energy is always conserved — it cannot be created or destroyed, only transferred between stores.
