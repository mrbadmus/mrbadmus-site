🎯 Quick Quiz
1. A charge of 30 C flows past a point in 6 seconds. What is the current?
Correct! I = Q ÷ t = 30 ÷ 6 = 5 A ✅
Not quite. Use I = Q ÷ t → I = 30 ÷ 6 = 5 A
2. Which component has a resistance that DECREASES as temperature increases?
Correct! A thermistor (NTC) has lower resistance at higher temperatures ✅
Not quite. A thermistor decreases in resistance as temperature rises — used in thermostats.
3. In a parallel circuit, what is the same across every branch?
Correct! P.D. is the same across all parallel branches ✅
Not quite. In a parallel circuit, the potential difference (p.d.) is the same across every branch.
