**Patterns.** Biggest issue is fake tier differentiation — Foundation and Higher are literally the same quiz (and FIFA set) on essentially every page, so every Higher/Triple page inherits Foundation's low demand. Second, counts are catastrophically low: ~90% of pages at 2–4 Qs. Where questions exist, distractor quality and register are strong (why/explain/predict framings, distractors encode real misconceptions: diffusion vs active transport, mitosis vs meiosis, arteries≠oxygenated). Fix priority is volume and tier-lift, not rewriting existing items.

**Not defects:** triple/monoclonal-antibodies (0 F / 3 H) and ecology/environmental-change (0 F / 2 H) correctly have empty Foundation quizzes — Triple/Higher-only spec points.
