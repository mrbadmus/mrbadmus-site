⚽ FIFA Worked Example
⚽ FIFA Example

In a mark-recapture experiment, 60 fish were caught and marked. Later, 50 fish were caught and 10 were marked. Estimate the total population.

F

Population = (n1 × n2) ÷ m

I

Population = (60 × 50) ÷ 10

F

Population = 3000 ÷ 10

A

Estimated population = 300 fish
