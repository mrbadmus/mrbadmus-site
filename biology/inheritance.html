Two parents are both carriers for cystic fibrosis (Ff). Calculate the probability of their child having cystic fibrosis.

F

Draw a Punnett square. Cystic fibrosis allele = f (recessive). Carrier = Ff

I

Ff × Ff → FF, Ff, Ff, ff

F

Only ff shows cystic fibrosis

A

Probability = 1 in 4 (25%)
