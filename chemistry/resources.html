The Haber process makes ammonia. State the conditions used and explain why a moderate temperature is chosen.

F

N₂ + 3H₂ ⇌ 2NH₃ (reversible reaction)

I

Conditions: 450°C, 200 atm, iron catalyst

F

High temp: faster rate but lower yield (forward reaction is exothermic). Compromise at 450°C

A

450°C gives a reasonable rate and acceptable yield — economic balance
